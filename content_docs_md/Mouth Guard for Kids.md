**Mouth Guard for Kids: Essential Sports Protection**

Children love sports, but dental injuries like broken or knocked-out teeth are common during physical activity. A mouth guard is a simple and effective way to protect your child’s teeth, gums, and jaw from injury.

**What Is a Mouth Guard?**

A mouth guard is a protective appliance worn over the teeth to absorb impact and prevent dental trauma. It is important for sports like football, basketball, cricket, martial arts, gymnastics, and skating.

**Why Do Kids Need It?**

A mouth guard helps to:
• Prevent chipped or broken teeth
• Reduce the risk of knocked-out teeth
• Protect lips, cheeks, and tongue
• Minimize jaw injuries

**Who Should Wear One?**

Children should wear a mouth guard if they:
• Play contact or team sports
• Participate in high-impact activities
• Wear braces (extra protection is essential)

**Benefits of Custom Mouth Guards**

• Better comfort and fit
• Superior protection
• Easier breathing and speaking
• More durable and effective

**Care Tips**

• Rinse after use
• Clean regularly
• Store in a ventilated case
• Avoid hot water
• Replace when worn or ill-fitting
