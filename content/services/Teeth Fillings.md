**Teeth Fillings: Restore Cavities & Protect Your Smile**

Tooth decay is a common dental problem that can lead to pain, infection, or tooth loss if untreated. Teeth fillings are a simple and affordable solution to repair cavities and restore your tooth’s function.

If you're searching for “tooth filling treatment near me” or “dental cavity filling cost,” here’s what you need to know.

**What Are Teeth Fillings?**

A dental filling repairs a tooth damaged by decay, cracks, or minor fractures. The decayed portion is removed and replaced with a filling material to restore shape and strength.

Fillings help:

- Stop further decay
- Restore chewing function
- Protect inner tooth layers
- Preserve natural tooth structure
Early treatment prevents more serious procedures like root canals.

**Signs You May Need a Filling**

- Sensitivity to hot or cold
- Pain while chewing
- Visible holes or dark spots
- Food getting stuck
- Mild toothache
Some cavities show no symptoms, so regular check-ups are important.

**Types of Fillings**

**Composite (Tooth-****Colored****) Fillings**

- Match natural teeth
- Ideal for visible areas
- Aesthetic option
**Glass Ionomer Fillings**

- Release fluoride
- Commonly used for children
Your dentist will recommend the best option based on durability, appearance, and budget.

**The Procedure**

The process usually takes 20–40 minutes and includes:

1. Examination
1. Removal of decay
1. Cleaning
1. Filling placement
1. Shaping and polishing
It’s typically completed in one visit.

**Are Fillings Painful?**

Modern treatments are comfortable and minimally invasive. Local anesthesia ensures a painless procedure, with mild temporary sensitivity afterward.

**Prevent Cavities**

- Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste
- Floss regularly
- Limit sugary foods
- Visit your dentist every 6 months
Prevention is always better than cure.
