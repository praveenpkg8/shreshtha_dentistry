**Dentures: Restore Your Smile & Confidence**

Missing multiple teeth can affect chewing, speaking, and self-confidence. Dentures offer a reliable and affordable way to replace missing teeth and restore oral function.

**What Are Dentures?**

Dentures are removable dental appliances that replace missing teeth and surrounding tissues. They are custom-made to fit comfortably and restore both function and appearance.

Dentures help:

- Improve chewing
- Enhance speech
- Support facial muscles
- Restore smile aesthetics
- Boost confidence
Modern dentures look natural and feel more comfortable than ever.

**Types of Dentures**

**Complete Dentures**

- Used when all teeth in the upper or lower jaw are missing
**Partial Dentures**

- Recommended when some natural teeth remain
**Implant-Supported Dentures**

- Secured with dental implants for better stability
Your dentist will recommend the best option after examination.

**Who Needs Dentures?**

You may benefit from dentures if you:

- Have lost multiple teeth
- Have severe gum disease
- Have advanced decay
- Need full mouth rehabilitation
- Want an affordable replacement option

**The Procedure**

1. Consultation and examination
1. Impressions of your mouth
1. Trial fitting
1. Final fabrication
1. Adjustments and follow-up
The goal is proper fit, comfort, and natural appearance.

**Benefits**

✔ Affordable tooth replacement
✔ Restores chewing and speech
✔ Improves facial appearance
✔ Non-surgical solution
✔ Custom-designed for comfort

**Comfort & Lifespan**

It may take a few weeks to adjust. Minor soreness is normal initially, but adjustments ensure comfort.

With proper care, dentures typically last **5–10 years**.

**Dentures vs Dental Implants**

- **Dentures:** Removable and cost-effective
- **Dental Implants:** Fixed, long-lasting, but require surgery
Your dentist will guide you toward the best choice based on your needs and budget
