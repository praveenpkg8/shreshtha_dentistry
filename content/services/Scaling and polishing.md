**Scaling & Polishing: The Key to Healthy Gums and a Brighter Smile**

Brushing and flossing are essential, but they cannot remove hardened tartar. **Scaling and polishing** is a professional dental cleaning treatment that keeps your teeth clean, gums healthy, and smile fresh.

**What Is Scaling & Polishing?**

Scaling and polishing is a preventive dental procedure that removes plaque, tartar (calculus), and stains from teeth.

**✔**** Scaling:** Removes hardened plaque above and below the gum line using ultrasonic instruments.
**✔**** Polishing:** Smooths the tooth surface and removes surface stains from coffee, tea, and smoking.

**Why Is Dental Scaling Important?**

Tartar buildup cannot be removed by brushing alone and can lead to:

- Gum inflammation (gingivitis)
- Bleeding gums
- Bad breath
- Gum disease (periodontitis)
- Tooth mobility in advanced cases
Regular scaling prevents these problems and protects long-term oral health.

**Benefits of Scaling & Polishing**

✔ Prevents gum disease
✔ Reduces bad breath
✔ Removes stains for a brighter smile
✔ Detects dental problems early
✔ Supports overall health

**How Often Should You Get Teeth Cleaning?**

Most dentists recommend **scaling and polishing every 6 months**. Patients with gum issues may need more frequent visits.

**Is Scaling Painful?**

The procedure is usually painless. Mild sensitivity may occur temporarily, but modern ultrasonic technology makes treatment quick and comfortable.

**What Happens During the Procedure?**

1. Dental examination
1. Ultrasonic scaling
1. Teeth polishing
1. Oral hygiene guidance
The treatment usually takes **20–40 minutes**.
